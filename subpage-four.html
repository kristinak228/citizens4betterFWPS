Planning for you child to go to college?

Pre-High School Checks

If you are planning on your child attending college there are several facts you need to know prior to your child entering high school.
Does your child have a graduation plan prior to entering high school? Many very successful high schools have these!

  1. High School Guidance. High school Guidance Counselors have approximately 400 students each and spend a good deal of their time working with struggling students. This leaves little time for serious planning of all students who think that they are headed for college.
  2. If financial aid is an issue it is very important that your student is a known quanity at schools that can afford them. This is a process that need to start in the 8th grade. It is not what college your student knows; it is what colleges know of your student. There are lots of colleges looking for Washington students!

There are monthly seminars on college planning in Federal Way.
