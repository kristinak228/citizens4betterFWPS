What can you do to improve Federal Way education?

Make Yourselves Heard

The five members of our School Board are, according to Washington law, responsible for the quality of our schools! If you have concerns about the information presented here, or elsewhere, we would suggest you arrange an appointment with a School Board Director! Their addresses are located on the Federal Way website of fwps.org.

They should be made aware of your concerns and you should request that they investigate the matter and that they, not an administrator, respond to you. This will ensure that they have a complete understanding of your concerns or suggestions.
